\subsubsection*{template$<$long embedded, typename real = double$>$class Polygon$<$ embedded, real $>$}

\-Class to represent a \hyperlink{class_polygon}{\-Polygon} in a \hyperlink{class_mesh}{\-Mesh}, embedded in 2\-D or 3\-D. 

\-It can be an element of the \hyperlink{class_mesh}{\-Mesh} in 2\-D, or a face of a \hyperlink{class_polyhedron}{\-Polyhedron} in 3\-D. \-The only way to initialize it is to call make\-\_\-shared\-\_\-\-Polygon \-Area, normal and centroid are computed during initialization and saved. \-Inheritance from enable\-\_\-shared\-\_\-from\-\_\-this to obtain a shared\-\_\-ptr from this.
